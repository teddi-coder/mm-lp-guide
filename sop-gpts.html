Notes on GPT use

  • Always prefer the client GPT over a platform GPT when one exists — client GPTs have the brand voice, location, and service details pre-loaded
  • LP pipeline replaces LP GPTs for new page builds — the Claude-powered pipeline generates copy automatically from the brief form. Use GPTs only as fallback or for manual sub-page builds
  • Ad copy GPTs are still in use — the Autopilot generates ad copy rewrites internally, but for new campaign copy at setup, use the GAds Copy Creator GPT
  • Store all GPT output in Google Drive — in the client's folder, named clearly with date and page/ad type
  • Human review is mandatory — all GPT output goes through Guy (initial review) and Teddi (buddy review) before anything goes live
